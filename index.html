Research

For my PhD awarded in May 2023, I wrote my dissertation on the origins, patterns, and consequences of somatic mutations in cancer, brain development, and gene editing.

As a postdoctoral fellow, I continue to study somatic mutations in these three settings by developing computational methods to detect somatic mutations from emerging technologies.
